XHTML vs HTML5

What is the difference between XHTML and HTML5? HTML5 is the new version with all of the changes and XHTML is a combination of HTML4 and XML 1.0 (the older version).

Absolute vs Relative URL's

What is the difference between absolute and relative url's? Absolute URL's contain more information. However relative URL's are more convenient because they are shorter and more portable.
